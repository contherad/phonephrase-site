Built for real parties

No app on the TV, no passing one phone around, nothing to sign up for.

Create a room, share a 4-letter code, and friends are in within seconds — everyone as an instant guest.

💣

A bomb you can feel

The hidden timer buzzes faster and harder as it gets close, ending in a full rumble on every phone in the room.

🛡️

Cheat-proof by design

The timer lives on the server and only the clue-giver's phone can ever see the word. No peeking.

AI word packs

🔁

Party-proof

12 word packs

Five ways to play

Same room, same code — five very different games.

💣

Classic

Hot potato with a hidden bomb. Guess it and the word leaps to the other team. Holder at boom loses the point.

🎭

Charades

Same bomb, but you act the word out. No talking!

🚫

Taboo

Describe the word without saying the forbidden words in red. Harder than it sounds.

⏱️

Race

Visible timer, every correct guess scores a point. Pure speed.

🥣

Fishbowl

Everyone secretly submits words, then the same deck gets played three ways: describe it, one word only, act it out.

🎲

Your rules

Score targets, easy or hard words, skip penalties that burn bomb time, custom team names, shuffle button for drama.
